AI Policies

Say No to AI

Our Stance

As bookish artists ourselves, we value art lead with human creativity, love and soul above all else. We have a very clear view that generative AI has no place in our community. This includes but is not limited too:

Character Art

Book Covers

Logos

Marketing

Products made with generative AI images

If the organisers are informed of generative AI use on the day, we reserve the rights to remove these items from being sold

The Vendors and Authors

We will never knowingly accept an Author or Vendor at our event who uses generative AI in any part of their work. Each application is carely considered and anyone found to be using generative AI will not be accepted.

Why it Matters

Years of practice, years of of training, years of research. Authors and Artists alike put their tears, heart and soul into their creations.

AI creates a space for plagerism, style theft and devalues the years of practice and refinement undertaken.

AI is leaving a heavy footprint on the world we also walk on.
