Our Policies

Everything you need to know before attending.

Tickets

All tickets are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled. If tickets are transferable, instructions will be provided closer to the event date.

Accessibility

Lost In Pages Events is committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming event. Accessibility information will be published once the venue has been confirmed. If you have specific requirements, please contact us before purchasing tickets.

Code of Conduct

We want everyone to enjoy a safe, welcoming experience. Harassment, discrimination, bullying or inappropriate behaviour towards attendees, authors, vendors, volunteers or staff will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the event.

Photography & Social Media

Photography and filming may take place throughout the event for promotional purposes. If you do not wish to appear in photographs, please speak to a member of staff on arrival.

Children

Children under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a responsible adult at all times.

Contact

Questions about our policies? Contact us at lostinpagesevents@gmail.com.
