Our Policies

Everything you need to know before attending.

Tickets & Attendance

All tickets are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled by us.

Accessibility

Accessibility is a human right. We believe that if our event is not accessible for everyone, it should not be accessible to anyone. All accessibility tickets include a free carer’s pass.

AI

We prioritise human creativity and storytelling in our work and aim to keep our creative process rooted in human expression and collaboration.

Code of Conduct

We want everyone to enjoy a safe, welcoming experience. Harassment, discrimination, bullying or inappropriate behaviour towards attendees, authors, vendors, volunteers or staff will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the event.

Photography & Social Media

Photography and filming may take place throughout the event for promotional purposes.

Contact

Questions about our policies? Contact us at lostinpagesevents@gmail.com.
