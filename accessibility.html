Accessibility Policies

Accessibility is for everyone.

Accessibility Tickets

We are committed to making our event as accessible as possible. If you need someone to attend with you to provide essential support, we offer one complimentary carers ticket.
We operate this scheme on trust and do not ask for medical evidence or proof of disability.
We simply ask that accessibility tickets are purchased when they are genuinely needed, so that we can continue to offer this to those who rely on it.

Venue Lines

Those with accessibility tickets will have their own line and entrance to the venue.
This helps ensure that access to the event is as seamless as possible.

Parking

There is disabled Parking at the following sites:

The NCP Brighton Centre Russell Road,

Regency Square Car Park

Churchill Square Green Car Park

All are within a 3 minute walk to the venue.
There is also limited disabled parking at the Hilton Hotel. Further information can be found on their site.

The Venue

We are working with accessibility advocates to ensure that not only is the venue fit for purpose, but also our plans. We want to ensure that everything is accessible for everyone. https://www.accessable.co.uk/hilton/access-guides/doubletree-brighton-metropole

Data Protection

By purchasing a ticket you consent to us securely storing your information for event administration, correspondence and promotional communications.

On the accessibility tickets, we do ask for basic information on your requirements. This information is used solely for the purpose of ensuring attendees can safely navigate and enjoy the event.

Quiet Space

We understand that book events can be overwhelming, and sometimes a quiet space away from the crowds is needed. We have allocated a large space within the event, where you can grab a sandwich and a drink, sit down and do some colouring. Alongside this, if you are looking to further escape the crowds without leaving the venue, there is an onsite sea view bar and terrace.

Feedback

Ensuring everyone is able to fully participate in our event is our top priority. We want you all to have the best time! If there is anything we can do to make your day more enjoyable, please reach out to us via email and we will do our best to accomodate.
