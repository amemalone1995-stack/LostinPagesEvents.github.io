Accessibility

Accessibility

Accessibility is for everyone

Accessibility Tickets
We are committed to making our event as accessible as possible. If you need someone to attend with you to provide eddential supoprt, we offer one complimentary carers ticket.
We operate this scheme on trust and do not ask for medical evidence or proof of disability.
We simply ask that accessibility tikets are purchased when they are genuinely needed, so that we can continue to offer this to those who rely on it.


Venue Lines

Those with accessibility tickets will have their own line and entrance to the venue
This helps ensure that access to the event is as seemless as possible

Parking

There is disabled Parking at the following sites: The NCP Brighton Centre Russell Road, Regency Square Car Park Churchill Square Green Car Park All are within a 3 minutes walk to the venue.
There is also limited disabled parking at the Hilton Hotel. Further information can be found on their site.

Conduct

We operate a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment, discrimination, verbal abuse, physical abuse or intimidation. Anyone removed from the event for unacceptable behaviour will not receive a refund.

Data Protection

By purchasing a ticket you consent to us securely storing your information for event administration, correspondence and promotional communications.

Your direct contact details will never be shared outside the event management team.

Attendance grants permission for photography and videography to be used for promotional purposes.

Age Restrictions

This is a 16+ event. Vendors have been asked to clearly identify any 18+ content.

Guests are responsible for following venue rules, fire safety procedures and emergency instructions.

Complaints & Compliments
